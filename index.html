🎓 Once College Starts — Choose Your Archery Slot

Archery does not stop when college starts. You pick which slot fits your college timetable. Both work. Pick one and stick to it.

Option A — Morning Archery

5:30–7:30 AMArchery practice. Done before the day begins. Non-negotiable.
8:00 AMGet ready, commute, college.
College hrsAttend every lecture. No bunking — ever.
6:00–8:00 PM2 focused study hours. CFA prep / Python / NISM depending on year.
After 8 PMRead one news item (Hindu Business Line). Then rest. Sleep by 10:30 PM.

✓ Best for people who are sharper in the morning and find evening studying easy.

Option B — Evening Archery

6:00–7:00 AMWake up, freshen up, light revision or news reading. No pressure.
College hrsAttend every lecture. Active participation — ask one question per class.
4:30–6:30 PM🏹 Archery practice. Evening slot. Consistent timing matters more than which slot.
7:30–9:30 PM2 focused study hours. CFA prep / Python / NISM depending on year.
After 9:30 PMWind down. Sleep by 11 PM maximum. Do not extend study past this.

✓ Best if college timings are early and morning feels rushed. Evening archery = natural decompression.

Both options deliver the same outcome — archery stays, CGPA stays, skill-building stays. The only question is which one you can maintain for 3 years without breaking. Pick once. Don't switch back and forth.

NISM — Your Finance Entry Ticket

NISM (National Institute of Securities Markets) certifications are mandatory for working in Indian capital markets — mutual funds, stock broking, research, derivatives. Low cost, high signal in finance internships and jobs.

✅ DO NISM SERIES V-A FIRST: It's the entry door to every finance internship. ₹1,500 exam fee. 100 MCQs. Pass rate ~65%. Do it in Year 1 Sem 2 alongside your Google Data Analytics cert.

Non-Negotiables — Every Single Day

✓

Archery — morning or evening, you decide once and don't change

See the two schedule options in the Gap Month section. Pick your slot before college starts and protect it for 3 years.

✓

2 focused study hours — phone in another room, not silent

Quality beats quantity every time. 2 real hours = 4 distracted hours. This is how CGPA and CFA prep happen simultaneously.

✓

One small Python / NISM / CFA task — not a session, one thing

One function. One chapter. One practice question. Compounding is invisible until it suddenly isn't.

✓

Read one financial news item — 5 minutes only

The Hindu Business Line or RBI website. Builds GK for CAT, RBI Grade B, and interviews. Start Year 1, not Year 3. This is passive CAT verbal prep running silently in the background.

✓

7 hours sleep — not a reward, a requirement

Sleep deprivation drops cognitive performance by 20–30%. You cannot absorb Economics or CFA material on 5 hours. Rest is preparation.

Things That Will Tempt You — Stay Alert

✗"I'll start seriously next semester" — the plan that never starts. Sem 1 CGPA is the hardest to recover.
✗Doing CFA + Actuarial + Python + 2 internships simultaneously in Year 2 — this is how people burn out and fail everything.
✗Dropping archery "just for this exam season" — it never comes back once you stop. Three months off = two years to rebuild.
✗Changing the plan every 6 months based on what someone else is doing. Their path is not your path. Trust this blueprint.
✗Measuring yourself against friends going to private MBA colleges or doing CA. Different race, different track. Comparison is noise.
